Introduction

Blackbody Research Consulting develops software that can automate complex problem solving.

We offer businesses and organizations a high-quality alternative to in-house research and development, leveraging machine learning techniques and open source software for:

  • Data Analytics
  • Task Automation
  • Image Recognition
  • Resource Planning/Optimization
  • Custom Solutions

Our Mission

Due to recent advances in machine learning, the intersection of applied statistics, computer science, and AI, we can now develop programs that learn on their own to solve complex and pressing problems in the fields of business, healthcare, and science.

As machine learning consultants, we use our technical expertise and experience to work alongside institutions to help them obtain valuable insights from their data, improve productivity, and save money.

Things We Do With Machine Learning:

Data Insights

By analyzing data, determine patterns and make predictions of future outcomes and trends.

Automation of Expert Tasks

Save time and money by automating repetitive manual and research tasks.

Predictive Analytics

Forecast with data what might happen in the future with an acceptable level of reliability and generate what-if scenarios and risk assessments.

Resource Planning/Logistics

Identify and resolve operational inefficiencies such as staffing/scheduling issues, budgetary waste, poor demand forecasting.

Image Recognition

Classify or identify objects automatically in pictures or documents.

Custom solutions

Design specialized machine learning solutions around a specific business or scientific problem.
